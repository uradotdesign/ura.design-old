Enigmail Rebranding

Enigmail is a seamlessly integrated security add-on for Mozilla Thunderbird. It uses OpenPGP to encrypt and digitally sign your emails and to decrypt and verify messages you receive.

Enigmail approached us to redesign their logo and user interface iconography, in time for the newest Enigmail 2.0 release. As one of the defacto PGP tools we hope to make email encryption more accessible and usable by far more people.
