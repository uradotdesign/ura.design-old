Full-stack developer (Identihub) - ReactJS + Laravel

Location: Remote

We are creating Identihub to make it easier to share assets and allow designers and developers to work closer together. As supporters of Open Source Design, we are looking to grow our team and establish that vision. We are looking for someone to grow with the Identihub team and understand the Open Source Design DNA.

About Ura Design

Ura is a small studio specialized in Open Source Design, working with and for open source projects to improve their Usability and Design. We are specialized on Visual Identity, Brand Strategy and generally UX Design catered to Open Source workflows (having both worked with Grassroots and Enterprise projects). In the past we have helped Mozilla, The Tor Project, The Linux Foundation, FSFE among many others.

Job Description

Ura is looking for a Fullstack Developer proficient in both Frontend (React) and Backend (Laravel) to maintain Identihub, our self-hosted brand and asset management open source software licensed under the AGPL. Identihub makes hosting and reusing your project’s visual assets easy by automating image conversions and offering advanced features for handling SVG files, colors, fonts and images. You can find more about it at: https://identihub.co
